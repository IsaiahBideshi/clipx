Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

We built ClipX to help you manage your clips with clarity and control. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account details like your email address and display name.
  • Clip metadata you choose to save, such as titles, tags, and timestamps.
  • Basic usage data that helps us keep ClipX stable and responsive.

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the ClipX experience.
  • Sync your clips across devices if you enable cloud features.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate product updates.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate ClipX, or when required by law.

Your choices

You can request access, updates, or deletion of your account information by contacting support. You can also choose which clips are synced or stored.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Please keep your account credentials secure.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out through the ClipX support channel.

Thanks for trusting ClipX with your best moments.
